Thank you for reviewing PetFlow. Please find the requested details below.

1. What does "테스터" mean?

"테스터" referred to approved pilot users who are helping us test PetFlow before a wider public launch. It does not refer to a paid role, a medical professional, or a separate class of users. To avoid confusion, we have updated the app-facing wording to use clearer terms such as "user", "required account information", and "participation code".

2. What does the "테스터 필수 정보" section do?

That section collected the minimum account information required after sign-in: nickname, Korean mobile phone number, and consent to collect those fields. The email address comes from the sign-in method. The phone number is not used for SMS verification, advertising, marketing, or tracking. It is used only for service notices, support, and test operation communication during the current pilot period. The label has been updated to "required account information" to make this clearer.

3. How can reviewers access the app's full features and services?

After signing in with the review account, please complete the required account information screen, add a pet, and create a health record. You can then access pet management, health records, image/video attachments, vaccination records, check score, 14-day health flow, hospital-sharing summaries, account linking, sign-out, and account deletion request flows.

The GPT-based veterinary review draft is available only after entering a participation code in the account screen. This restriction is used to control AI API costs and to ensure the AI draft is clearly limited to an unreviewed veterinary handoff draft. The feature does not provide diagnosis, prescriptions, medication names, dosage, or treatment plans.

If you need access to the GPT draft feature during review, please use the participation code provided in App Review Notes or contact us through this review thread and we will provide an active review code.
